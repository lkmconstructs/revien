Decision: [[Halcyon Web]] is self-hosted on the [[Atlas Server]], not deployed to
Vercel or any edge platform.

## Rationale
Sovereignty over convenience: one box we own beats a platform we rent. Influenced
by [[Reading - Local-First Software]]. Latency cost measured at 40ms p50 — acceptable.
